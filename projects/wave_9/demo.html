Demonstration

The wave9 mobile app experience is premised on three experiential layers

Custom Map

Using GPS location services, teh viewer is placed along the model's path, and provided information about upcoming stops along the walking tour. The map experience is augmented with ruminations about the nature of our solar system, urging the reinterprtation of the model's physical environment from the banal to the sublime representation of solar distance.

Contextualization Detail

Navigationally accessible from both the map and the augmented layer, the contextualization detail provides viewers with information about the planet specific marker locations, size and distance of the planets within the model.

Augmented Reality Viewer

audiences encounter various markers which trigger 3D renderings within the mobile app visualizing the various planets of our solar system using augmented reality technology.
