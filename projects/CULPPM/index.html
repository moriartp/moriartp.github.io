STATES

Initiating
Projects in the initiating stage are addressed at a high level. Focus is on defining project goals and stakeholder identification.

In Planning
Projects in the Planning stage are addressed at all levels. Focus is on defining Scope, Schedule, Budget, and Risk.

Executing
Projects in the Executing stage are focussed on value creation efforts, building toward project deliverables. Additionally focused on providing work performance metrics for monitoring and control purposes.

Closing
Projects in the Closing stage are focused on transitions to service, and gathering insights for future process improvement efforts.

Archived
Projects in the Archived stage represent that completed effort and may serve of historical records and organization assets for reference in future effort and planning.

STATUS

Initiating
Articulation and framing of project goals in progress.

In Planning
Project plan is actively being developed.

Future
Future projects represent projects in a suspended state of execution, where all value-creating work activities are scheduled to begin at a future date.

On Target
Execution of project plan is meeting expectations.

At Risk
Some aspect of scope, schedule, or resource utilization may not conform to planned efforts in a way that potentially compromises project success.

On Hold
A suspended state of execution, requiring some redefinition of scope, schedule, or resource allocation.

Breached
Project is in Closing state, all work is complete, and some aspect of scope, cost, or timeliness was not succesfully achieved.

Completed
100% of planned work completed and project has been archived.

Canceled
Archived projects were less than 100% of work is completed, and remaining work will NOT be pursued.
