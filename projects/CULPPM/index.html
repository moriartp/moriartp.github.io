Project Name

 		Grant Sponsor

The Project Sponsor is responsible for initiating projects, defining project goals, and approbation of planning documents at various validation checkpoints.

 		Business Owner The business owner is responsible for providing functional expertise as it relates to the business impact of a new service or product. The business owner will be key in Scoping processes, the identification of influencer stakeholders, deliverable acceptance, and product service testing. Business owners will reliably contribute directly to project work. Project Manager The Project Manager is responsible for all aspects of a project throughout all lifecycle states, ensuring a proper process flow and effective monitoring and control processes. Class Ordinal classification of projects based on relative complexity and value to the Libraries from 1 (smaller projects) to 3 (larger projects). State

STATES

Initiating
Projects in the initiating stage are addressed at a high level. Focus is on defining project goals and stakeholder identification.

In Planning
Projects in the Planning stage are addressed at all levels. Focus is on defining Scope, Schedule, Budget, and Risk.

Executing
Projects in the Executing stage are focussed on value creation efforts, building toward project deliverables. Additionally focused on providing work performance metrics for monitoring and control purposes.

Closing
Projects in the Closing stage are focused on transitions to service, and gathering insights for future process improvement efforts.

Archived
Projects in the Archived stage represent that completed effort and may serve of historical records and organization assets for reference in future effort and planning.
