The 2015 Federal Employee Viewpoint Survey collected 0ver 400,000 responses from federal employees to learn about the culture, work environments, and levels of overall satisfaction experience by agency employees.

By aggregating information provided by individual employees we can understand the organizational strengths and weaknesses throughout the government and understand what factors contribute to employment satisfaction.

With this information, strategic decisions may be made that will address organizational shortcomings as identified by the very people who make up our administrative offices, and work to increase overal levels of satisfaction with federal agencies.
