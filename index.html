clown nosing

A demonstration of p5.js interaction and mouse event triggers. Simple, playful, just grab the clown nose as instructed, drag it around the screen, and push the other clown noses around in various directions. view demo.

interstellar traveler

This visualization provides 24 slides views, one for each celestial hour. select a transport vehicle from the carousel, and hover over the stars. The interactions display the celestial coordinates for each star, its distances from the sun and estimated duration of travel, based on the average top speed of the selected transport vehicle. view demo.

text clouds

On September 25th 2015, countries adopted a set of goals to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all as part of a new sustainable development agenda. Each goal has specific targets to be achieved over the next 15 years. Through click iteration, this visualization provides text animations of UN sustainability objectives. view demo.
